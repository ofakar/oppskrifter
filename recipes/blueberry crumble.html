# Blueberry Crumble ### **Ingredients** #### **For the Blueberry Filling** - **500 g** blåbær - **50 g** sukker - **1 tbsp** sitronjuice (**~15 g**) - **2 tsp** maizena (**~5 g**) - **1 tsp** vaniljeekstrakt (**~5 g**, optional) #### **For the Crumble Topping** - **100 g** mel - **80 g** store havregryn - **80 g** brunt sukker - **1/2 tsp** kanel - **100 g** smør - **Pinch of salt**
